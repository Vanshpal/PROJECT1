SHOOT SPEED RUN

Defeat the monsters strategically. Take down the boss to retrieve the esential artifact. Have the fastest time out of all your friends! HERE ARE SOME TIPS:

  1. Hold right click to block (Max holding = 5 seconds) (1 second cooldown)
  2. Water & Bushes are used to hide
  3. Press E to heal once you have defeated the first monster (from the drop)
  4. Avoid lava
  5. Collect the monsters' drops
If on a tablet or phone, simply rotate the device in order to collect the items. If on a desktop, use the mouse and arrows keys to collect the items.
